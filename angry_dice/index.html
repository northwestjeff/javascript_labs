Angry Dice

The object is to complete all three rounds, in order. Players can roll both die or hold a die to help them roll that Round's pair (except 6's cannot be held). TWO ANGRY FACES RESTARTS THE GAME!

  • Round 1 - Die 1 and Die 2
  • Round 2 - Angry and Die 4
  • Round 3 - Die 5 and Die 6
  • If you get two Angry Dice you must start at round 1 again.
